## Summary

HealthGuard AI is an AI-powered decision-support system designed to identify high-risk patients early and support healthcare workers in prioritizing care. By analyzing health and demographic data, the project helps allocate limited medical resources more efficiently, particularly in low-resource settings. The goal is to enable proactive interventions, improve health outcomes, and promote equitable access to healthcare while respecting privacy and responsible AI principles.# HealthGuard-AI
